As mentioned in the official website of Tesla, Tesla Model 3 is one of the safest car of the world. It has High impect protection, rigid body structure and very low rollover risk.

Tesla Model 3 Autopilot System

Tesla offers you the most advanced and futuristic autopilot feature in model 3.

An autopilot or simply we can say it as self driving, is a system with which the car can drive itself without requiring constant manual control by a human operator. So it’s amazing! Right? Your car can drive to your destination even if you are sleeping.

The Tesla autopilot can automatically steer, accelerate and brake in the road. The most important thing is that Tesla is improving it day by day to make it fully automatic.



The features of the Tesla autopilot is -
1. Active guidance on-ramp to off-ramp
2. Autoparking
3. Retrieve your car automatically.
4. Change the track automatically on the highway.

Tesla claims that “self-driving in almost all circumstances, at a safety level we believe will be at least twice as good as the average human driver. “
